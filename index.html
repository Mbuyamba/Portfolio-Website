Adoption of Soft Contact Lenses in Myopia Reduction

According to estimates, by 2050, half of the world's population will have myopia. Given the deleterious effect of myopia on the quality of life, reducing its progression, particularly in children, with an easily accessible solution will positively impact society while providing financial incentives to eye care providers and contact lens manufacturers.

In this project, I reviewed the data of various studies using soft multifocal contact lenses to reduce myopia progression. I found that soft contact lenses effectively slow down myopia's progression.
